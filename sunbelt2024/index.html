Collaboration Networks

A collaboration network is a social structure composed of individuals or organizations connected through collaborative interactions, such as co-authorships, partnerships, or joint projects. These networks facilitate the sharing of resources, knowledge, and skills to achieve common goals and enhance collective performance.


Typical collaboration networks are

  • “scale-free”

  • assortative

  • small worlds

Collaboration and Competition Networks


Collaboration Networks (teams ports)
two players have collaborated if they were active for the same team during a specific time window such as a whole season.


Competition Networks (individual sports)
two players have competed if they played against each other in a competition.
